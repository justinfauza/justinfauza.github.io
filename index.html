Assemble PC (LAB)

Our initial lab focused on PC assembly and proved to be an enjoyable and laid-back assignment. The task involved disassembling various PC components, including removing the cover, PSU, cables, RAM, and HDD. Subsequently, we reassembled the components, starting with the HDD, followed by the RAM, Power Supply, reconnecting all cables, and closing the cover. After completion, we tested whether the PC turned on without any issues, and most of us succeeded on the first attempt. Following the practical assembly, we took a brief quiz covering the components and the disassembly process. While I had prior experience with DIY PC projects, it was evident that this hands-on activity was an exciting and valuable experience for beginners.
